The Proof
Quick wins that immediately make your profile look sharp.
Experience section: achievements, not duties
Bad

"Responsible for sales in the regional team."

Good

"Increased regional sales 20% by implementing a new CRM workflow that cut response time in half."

30-second quick win: Custom URL

Change your default LinkedIn URL from a random string of numbers to your name.

linkedin.com/in/12345 → linkedin.com/in/yourname

Looks professional. Improves SEO. Takes 30 seconds. Do it today.

What to actually post
The 4 Pillars of LinkedIn Content
Pick one of these every time you post. You'll never run out of ideas.

Product & Innovation

Share launches, feature updates, behind-the-scenes thinking, and company milestones. You're building something cool. Show it.

Industry Insights

Your analysis and commentary on what's happening in your field. Share your perspective on news, trends, and shifts you're seeing.

Hot Takes

"Unpopular opinion..." or "Stop doing X." Contrarian views spark conversation and get huge reach. Don't be afraid to have a POV.

Personal Updates

Hobbies, travels, lessons from life. Humanise your brand while connecting back to professional values. People follow people.
